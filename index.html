Who I am

Curious by nature — a dilettante, in the best sense.

JCadd headshot.jpg

I'm curious to a fault — the kind of person who has to understand how a thing actually works before I'll let it go. That instinct has pulled me through a few different worlds rather than one neat career path, and I wouldn't have it any other way.

My background is genuinely multidisciplinary, anchored by a first-class honours degree in sport and exercise science. I like going deep on a subject, then carrying what I've learned somewhere unexpected. A bit of a dilettante — happiest exploring a new industry and figuring out what makes it tick.

That's how Vault came about. I needed to track my own finances while earning in AUD inside a British system, and nothing out there did it properly — so I built it. Find a real problem, build the thing that fixes it. Now I do exactly that for other businesses: automation tools that quietly take the tedious work off their plate.
